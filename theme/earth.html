The bright light in the background is the sun, a massive burning star rising over earth. The sun is a sphere of hot plasma that accounts for 99.86% of the mass in our solar system and is the star at the center of our solar system. The immediate planet is earth, the planet we thrive in. It is the third closest planet to the sun and is approximately 4.5 billion years old. It is the only known planet that contains living organisms to date.
